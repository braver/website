About

I'm a designer/programmer based in Delft, The Netherlands. Photography is my artistic outlet.

The water is never far away in my part of The Netherlands and I guess it shows in the photographs. So much of my youth was spent sailing, it just becomes a part of who you are.

It's also a way of slowing down, being in the moment. F/8 and be there, like really be there. My style is seeped in tradition, shot on Tri-X without manipulation or cropping. I want to stay true to what I saw, without bringing something preconceived into it.

Also check Instagram for mostly random stuff.
